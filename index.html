Blackbirds

Synthwave High Fantasy

Set in the world of Ninesilver, Blackbirds pits players for or against the State of Brillante, crown jewel of the Saint Iris Tribe, the most powerful government in the world.

Over one thousand years have passed since a terrible war that ruined the surface of the planet. Ninesilver has recovered - and in its earthen flesh are great and terrible secrets.
